About

Hi, I'm Ben Ajayi-obe

I am a results focused, data scientist who enjoys building effective data solutions to business problems. I am personally interested in Deep Learning and NLP tasks, but have commerical experience in a wide range of machine learning problems.

I am also a Engalnd Council Member for the MS Society, where I look after the Thame Valley Region and assist in the operation of the charity.

I also take part in ultra-distance endurance events, such as the Talisker Whisky Atlantic Challenge and Land's End to John O'Groats. I also hold a world record for the overall longest continual row by an individual of any weight class on an indoor rowing machine. I'm available as a speaker for corporate events.
